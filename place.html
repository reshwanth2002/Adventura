About

Get swept away by the atmosphere of romance that permeates Visakhapatnam with this walking tour. Led by a professional local guide, discover the cities highlights. Learn where to go on dates, take photos, and how to navigate the streets, buildings and parks of the city. The tour includes a visit to Kailasagiri and the retelling of heartbreaking, tragic and spicy love stories.
